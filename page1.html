A champion is a character that is chosen by each player. Each champion has various unique abilities and different stats. Each champion also performs certain roles such as absorbing damage, dealing physical damage, dealing magic damagic, etc. You can buy items to increase your champion's stats. Below is an example of the champion Ryze.
Ryze is considered a mage. This means he tends to use his abilities to damage. He will try to upgrade his Mana and Ability Power stats through items.

(Passive)Arcane Mastery: Converts a portion of Ryze's maximum mana into ability power.
(Q)Overload: Does damage to the first enemy hit in the direction this aimed at.
(W)Rune prison: Immobilizes a targeted enemy champion for a short duration
(E)Spell flux: Marks a targeted enemy and all enemies within a short radius of the targeted enemy and does damage. When Ryze uses his (Q) or (W) on a marked enemy, it consumes the mark and gains bonus effects.
(R)Realm warp: Creates a teleportation circle circle at Ryze's current location and at a targeted location. After a brief delay, all ally units standing in the circle created at Ryze's current location get teleported to the targeted location.
