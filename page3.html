There are various items that are available. Each item raises one or more stats and some may even have special abilities. Items can be categorized in many different ways and some categories overlap. A broad categorization can be done using 3 categories: Offensive items, defensive items, and support items.
Category Sample items Description
Offensive Items These items usually boosts a character's attack damage, ability power, attack speed, critical strike chance, or a combination of those stats by a significant amount.
Defensive Items These items usually boosts a character's armor, magic resistance, health, or a combination of those stats by a significant amount
Support Items These items gives extra gold income and can be offensive or defensive. In additon, these items usually boost offensive or defensive stats by a small amount.
