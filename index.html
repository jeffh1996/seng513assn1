Description: League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena(MOBA) video game developed by Riot Games Inc. Released in 2009, League of Legends has become one of the top MOBA games played around the world.

Gameplay: The normal game mode of League of Legends is played on a map called Summoner's Rift. It consists of two teams of 5 players each. Each player selects a champion which they will be controlling for the duration of the games. The objective of the game is to destroy the enemy's nexus which is a building in their base.
