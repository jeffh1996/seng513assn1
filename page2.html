The main map used for gameplay is the summoner's rift map shown below. There are two sides for two opposing teams. A diagonal line can be drawn down the middle to separate the blue team and the purple team.

There are 3 lanes and a jungle on each side. Typically 2 people go to the bottom lane, and 1 person goes to the remaining 3 places (top, jungle, mid).
