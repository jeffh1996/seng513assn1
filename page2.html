The main map used for gameplay is the summoner's rift map shown below. There are two sides for two opposing teams. A diagonal line can be drawn down the middle to separate the blue team and the purple team.

There are 3 lanes and a jungle on each side. Typically 2 people go to the bottom lane, and 1 person goes to the remaining 3 places (top, jungle, mid).

The objective of the game is to destroy the nexus in the enemy's base which is represented by the white circle. Each lane is protected by 3 turrets which are very difficult to destroy as well as one inhibitor which is represented by the white diamonds.
Each lane spawns minions from each team's nexus. When a player lands a killing blow on an enemy minion they will receive gold which they can be used to buy items. Each team also has a jungle which contain neutral monsters (any player can kill them). They are much harder to kill than lane minions (but certain champions can still do it by themselver) can but drop more gold.

The two team's jungle is separated by an area called the river. The river contains two powerful monsters which usually requires more than one player (or even requires an entire team) to take down. These monsters grant powerful effects to the entire team of the player who lands the killing blow.
